About Me

I'm currently a junior at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor studying Computer Science with a minor in User Design Experience.

I grew up in Dalian, China, and am currently based in Chicago, IL. My love for computers and video games started when I was a kid, thanks to my uncle, who was also a Computer Science major and a big gamer. Growing up surrounded by his passion for technology, I quickly developed my own fascination with how computers work and how design shapes our experience with technology.

Outside of technology related work, I love listening to music. A night stroll with headphones on is the perfect way to clear my mind, or to think about code. I also did Chinese dance for 14 years since the first time it caught my eyes on TV when I was four years old!
